Our Mission

Every Startup Story
Deserves to Be Told

Startup Decoding was born from a simple belief — that behind every successful startup is a human story worth telling. What started as a passionate media venture grew into a community of millions. Our flagship Instagram page reached 750K followers before being banned — but that didn't stop us. We rebuilt stronger, with News Decoding (300K), Startups Unboxing (200K), and a growing presence on LinkedIn, Twitter, and YouTube. Today, Startup Decoding is India's dedicated platform for decoding startup journeys, celebrating entrepreneurial grit, and connecting founders with stories that inspire.
